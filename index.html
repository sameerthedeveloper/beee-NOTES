BEEE Semester Study Notes

Module 1: Basic Circuit Laws and Analysis

Mesh Analysis: Apply KVL: \( \sum V = 0 \)
Nodal Analysis: Use KCL: \( \sum I = 0 \), \( I = \frac{V}{R} \)
Ohm's Law: \( V = IR \), where \( R = \frac{\rho l}{A} \)
Series Circuits: \( R_{eq} = R_1 + R_2 + \ldots \)
Parallel Circuits: \( \frac{1}{R_{eq}} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \ldots \)
Star-Delta Conversion: Use transformation formulas for complex networks.

Module 2: AC Circuits

RL Circuit: \( V = IR + L\frac{dI}{dt} \), \( I(t) = \frac{V}{R}(1 - e^{-\frac{R}{L}t}) \)
RC Circuit: \( V = IR + \frac{1}{C} \int I dt \), \( I(t) = \frac{V}{R} e^{-t/RC} \)
RLC Circuit: \( L \frac{d^2I}{dt^2} + R \frac{dI}{dt} + \frac{I}{C} = \frac{dV}{dt} \)
Power Factor: \( \cos\theta = \frac{R}{Z} \), where \( Z = \sqrt{R^2 + (X_L - X_C)^2} \)

Module 3: Machines

DC Generator EMF: \( E = \frac{P \Phi Z N}{60 A} \)
DC Motor: \( E_b = \frac{P \Phi Z N}{60 A} \), and \( T \propto \Phi I_a \)
Transformer EMF: \( E = 4.44 f N \Phi_m \), and \( \frac{E_1}{E_2} = \frac{N_1}{N_2} \)

Module 4: Semiconductor Devices

PN Junction: Shockley equation: \( I = I_0(e^{\frac{V}{\eta V_T}} - 1) \)
Zener Diode: Operates in reverse breakdown region with stable voltage
BJT Operation: \( I_C = \beta I_B \); modes: active, saturation, cut-off

Module 5: Digital Electronics

SR Flip-Flop: \( Q_{next} = S + \overline{R}Q \)
JK Flip-Flop: \( Q_{next} = J\overline{Q} + \overline{K}Q \)
Half Adder: Sum = \( A \oplus B \), Carry = \( AB \)
Full Adder: Sum = \( A \oplus B \oplus C_{in} \), Carry = \( AB + BC_{in} + AC_{in} \)
A/D & D/A Converters: Flash comparator and R-2R Ladder logic
